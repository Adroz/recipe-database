# Taco Seasoning
* Serves: 4
* Time required: 10 mins
* Origin: Kirk Woll

## Ingredients
* 4 tbsp chili powder
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp onion powder
* 1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 2 tsp paprika
* 2 tbsp ground cumin
* 4 tsp sea salt
* 4 tsp black pepper

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and store in an airtight contianer.
