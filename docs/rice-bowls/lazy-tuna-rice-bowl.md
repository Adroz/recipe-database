# Lazy Tuna Rice Bowl
* Serves: 1
* Time required: 10 mins
* Origin: Healthy Recipes for Lazy People 2026

## Ingredients
* 1 can tuna, drained well
* light mayonnaise
* teriyaki sauce
* sriracha, to taste
* salt, to taste
* garlic powder, to taste
* onion powder, to taste
* 1 green onion, chopped
* cooked rice (microwavable sticky rice recommended)
* cucumber, sliced (optional)
* avocado, sliced
* sesame seeds
* teriyaki seaweed snacks

## Method
1. Drain the canned tuna (`1 can`) very well to remove all liquid.
2. In a bowl, mix the drained tuna with light mayonnaise, teriyaki sauce, a little sriracha, salt (`to taste`), garlic powder (`to taste`), and onion powder (`to taste`).
3. Prepare or heat up your rice in the microwave according to package directions.
4. Add the rice to a serving bowl.
5. Add chopped green onion (`1`) on top of the rice.
6. Top with the seasoned tuna mixture.
7. Add sliced avocado on the side.
8. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
9. Serve with cucumber slices (optional) and teriyaki seaweed snacks on the side.
